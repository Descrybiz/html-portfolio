The Best Movies According to David

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time

The Matritx

A sci-fi movie that really liberated my mind and made me believe that anything is truly possible as long as I put my mind to it.

Spider-Man: No Way Home

As being a huge Spider-Man fan, I found the message of "always helping people regardless of their past" resonated with me deeply.

It's a Wonderful Life

It's an old black and white film that taught me that life may not always go your way but there are still plenty of things to be grateful for.
